Cinnamon Bread

Description

This apple cinnamon bread recipe is super easy to make and so flavorful. Grating two large apples into the batter gives the bread a fresh apple flavor and makes your kitchen smell amazing!

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon and Spices
  • Oil
  • Eggs
  • Bakin Powder and Baking Soda
  • Vanilla
  • Nuts

Steps

  1. Ket grated apples sit with the sugar, salt and spices
  2. After 10 to 15 minutes, stir the remaining ingredients, pour the batter into a loaf pan and bake
  3. When the bread is done, let it cool completely
  4. Done
